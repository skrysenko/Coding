Geoinformproekt LLC is a team of professionals representing the mix of youth and experience.
The history of our company began in 2000, when employees of the research laboratory of Land Managementand GIS at the Chernihiv State Institute of Economics and Management CSIEM decided to create a company to promote GIS technologies for application in solving practical problems, in particular, for composing of landregister and development of geodatabases for the further applying in practical training for students at thedepartments of Land Management and Cadastre and Geographic Information Systems and Technologies as wellas a base for student graduate works.

More

We have first started to use geodetic GPS-systems, ArcInfo (ArcGIS) and ERDAS Imaging software, remotesensing materials and Internet / Intranet-technologies in the Chernihiv region. We do not focus only on land management. Over the years, we have developed a lot of practical techniquesand gained invaluable experience in dealing with a wide range of objectives (from the creation of commonthematic and topographic maps to implement system tasks) and a large database of geoinformation. Our employees combine practical activities with teaching and research, taking part in international and Ukrainian scientific-practical conferences as well as in scientific work at the department of Geographic Information Systems and Technologies at the Chernihiv State Institute of Economics and Management and implementing research programs funded by the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine. Scientific, educational and practical activities have been combined in our company for many years. Manystudents have done their practical works in Geoinformproekt LLC. And some of them stayed as employees. With the development of our company it has become difficult to combine different activities, and preferencewas given to the practical one. Today, we are focused on solving of various problems, but our commitment remains the same, namely the GISand the desire to be at the forefront of this technologies.

Services, provided by Geoinformproekt LLC:

  • Geoprocessing

    • LLC "Geoinformproekt" conducts its activities in accordance with a license to conduct land surveying works. LLC "Geoinformproekt" has extensive experience in land management and performs all types of land management work any complexity:
    - projects of allotment for the installation (renewal) of the boundaries of the land plot in kind (on the ground);
    - land management projects to organize existing land holdings;
    - documentation on land management for the formation of land plots;
    - projects of allotment of land plots for various purposes;
    - technical documentation on land inventory;
    - crop rotation projects;
    - documentation on the re-registration of ownership of land in the process of inheritance, donation, etc.

  • Land valuation

    • LLC "Geoinformproekt" conducts its activities in accordance with a license to conduct land appraisal works series

    Geoinformproject LLC has extensive experience in land appraisal and performs the following works:
    - expert assessment of land plots;
    - normative assessment of land plots.

  • GIS-Cartography

    • LLC "Geoinformproject" has extensive experience in performing work on the integration of heterogeneous spatially coordinated information into a single geospatial database, creation of digital and electronic maps of varying complexity and purpose. When performing work, materials of remote sensing of the Earth are widely used. We work with leading suppliers of space and aerial imagery. The work is carried out using both domestic and foreign software, such as:
    - Digital;
    - ArcGIS;
    - ERDAS;
    - Quantum GIS
    etc.

  • Geodesy

    • LLC "Geoinformproekt" has extensive experience in performing topographic and geodetic works of any complexity using modern geodetic equipment. The work is carried out with a combined method - tacheometric survey using GPS and digital terrain models.
